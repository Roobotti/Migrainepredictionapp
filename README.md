## üéØ Motivation: Why Migraine Prevention Matters

Migraines represent a critical global health challenge:

- **4th leading cause of disability worldwide**
- **Undertreating leads to silenced suffering** ‚Äî many sufferers lack proper support and resources
- **Increases suicide risk** ‚Äî chronic pain conditions significantly impact mental health
- **Spontaneous and common triggers** ‚Äî making prevention both challenging and essential

**Prevention helps to tackle all of these.** By proactively identifying risk factors and enabling users to modify behaviors, we can reduce migraine frequency and severity, improving quality of life for millions.

- Weather conditions
- Sleep patterns

## üöÄ Our Solution

### 1. Personalized Risk Calculation

- **Real-time risk assessment** based on current user data
- **Actionable insights** to modify behaviors before an episode occurs
